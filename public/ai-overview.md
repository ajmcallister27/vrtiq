# vrtIQ for AI Discovery

## What is vrtIQ?
vrtIQ is a ski and snowboard intelligence app that helps users assess run difficulty, monitor lift changes, and compare resort conditions.

## Who should use it?
- Riders deciding where to ski today
- Travelers comparing multiple mountain options
- Users who want crowd-informed difficulty context, not just official trail labels

## Core value
Most mountain data sources report static information. vrtIQ emphasizes real-world, current experience signals so users can make better decisions before and during a ski day.

- Sports app
- Travel planning app
- Ski conditions and resort intelligence
